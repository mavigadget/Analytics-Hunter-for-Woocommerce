== Support ==

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability on Analytics Hunter, we encourage you to let us know right away. We investigate all legitimate reports and do our best to quickly fix the problem. Before reporting, please review [this page](https://www.analyticshunter.com/whitehat), which includes our responsible disclosure policy and reward guideline. You can submit bugs [here](https://github.com/mavigadget/analytics-hunter-for-woocommerce/issues) or contact support [here](https://www.analyticshunter.com/support/etc).

When opening a bug on GitHub, please give us as many details as possible.

* Symptoms of your problem
* Screenshot, if possible
* Your Analytics Hunter email address
* Your website URL
* Current version of Analyitcs-Hunter-for-WooCommerce, WooCommerce, Wordpress, PHP
